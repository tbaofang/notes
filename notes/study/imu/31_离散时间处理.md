粒子在坐标系中$z=h$中的平面做圆周运动,坐标为:$r=(a\cos\theta, a \sin \theta, h)^T$,对坐标求导得:

$$
\begin{aligned}
  \dot{r} &= (-a \dot{\theta} \sin \theta, a\dot{\theta}\cos\theta, 0)^T \\ 
  &= \begin{bmatrix}
    0 & -\dot{\theta} & 0 \\
    \dot{\theta} & 0 & 0 \\
    0 & 0 & 0 \\
  \end{bmatrix}
  \begin{bmatrix}
    a\cos\theta \\ a\sin\theta \\ h
  \end{bmatrix} \\
  &= w \times r
\end{aligned}
$$
